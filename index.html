We specialise in a variety of steel products which we buy from Tata Steel UK in South Wales, United Kingdom.

Electrolytic Tinplate in coils/sheets:

  • Single reduced and double reduced steel grades – material is tempered to match certain strength and ductility requirements
  • thickness (gauge)
  • coating weight
  • surface finish – to meet your processing needs and product performance requirements
  • width - up to 1200mm for optimised operational flexibility and output
  • oils – for protection in transit

Tin Free Steel in coils/sheets:

  • TFS is packaging steel that is available in a wide choice of grades, thickness, surface finish and width
  • Electrolytic chromium coated steel (ECCS) for packaging is well-proven in a range of applications. Providing an excellent substrate for lacquering, this tin-free steel is available in a wide choice of grades, thickness and surface finish to suit your processes and applications

Protact (Ferrolite) PVC laminated, Polymer coated steel in coils/sheets:

  • Infinitely recyclable, Protact is food-safe and optimised for efficient canmaking. It is a proven material solution across a number of high-performance canmaking applications and formats including DRD cans and D&I aerosols.
