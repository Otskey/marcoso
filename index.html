About Us

Marcoso Global Resources Ltd is a trading company with a combination of 25+ years of industry experience.

arrow_circle_right

We source good quality and specific grades of special steels from our internationally recognised and renowned steel producing partners to supply and service our vast international clientele base.

arrow_circle_right

Some of these products include; Electrolytic Tinplate, Chrome Coated Steel/ Tin Free Steel and Protact/Laminated Steel. We can also source Hot Rolled Steel, Cold Rolled Steel, Galvanised Steel, Black Plate, Pre Painted Steel

arrow_circle_right

All logistics relating to the buying/selling of our products is kept in house. With this approach we have established strong relationships within the freight forwarding/shipping industry and can now offer bespoke logistical support/resolutions to others.

Sourcing

Given our many years working closely with various suppliers across the world, we are confident that we can meet specific customer requirements.

Communication

Strategic Growth

It is important to us that we grow as a business and with our trading partners. Our aim is to ensure that we are providing our goods and services to those who also take the same approach.

Global Presence
