Electrolytic Tinplate in coils/sheets

Electrolytic Tinplate Coils and sheets are used primarily for food packaging. The tin is typically coated by an electrolytic deposition. This coating provides a non-toxic barrier to protect food products from impurities and bacteria.

Tin Free Steel in coils/sheets

Tin Free Steel (TFS) is Electrolytic Chromium Coated Steel (ECCS) which is a high-quality alternative to tinplate. The chromium coating offers protection against corrosion as well as being alkaline and PH neutral and therefore can be used in food packaging and/or for paint cans.

Protact/laminated Steel

Protact is polymer (film) coated which is BPA-free and suitable for food packaging. It is proven to be efficient in can making.
