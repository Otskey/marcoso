Our Products

Given our many years of experience in the international steel markets and industries, we have been able to unify various suppliers with new outlets for the products that they offer. Through this established network we source both prime and non-prime (secondary grade) materials to support our customers covering (mainly) a combination of the SAMENA regions (South Asia, Middle East and North Africa). Using both our experience and strengths, we have been able to apply a similar approach to products away from steel, such as petrochemicals.

How Marcoso can assist your business

We source good quality and specific grades of special steels from our internationally recognised and renowned steel producing partners to supply and service our vast international clientele base.

Electrolytic Tinplate

Uses for this material include: Food Cans, Beverage Cans, Aerosol Cans and Aerospace.

Tin free steel/Electrolytic chromium coated steel

Uses for this material include: Food cans, Paint cans, Caps/Lids and Jewellery manufacture.

Protact/laminated steel

Uses for this material include: Aerosol cans, Paint & industrial, Food & beverage and Lids/Rings.

Galvanised Steel

Uses for this material include: Nuts, bolts, nails, Outdoor piping, Benches/ladders and Construction industry.

Hot Rolled Steel

Uses for this material include: Pipes & tubes, Railroad tracks, Doors & shelving, Automobile industry and Compressor chambers.

Cold Rolled Steel

Uses for this material include: Automotive industry, Agriculture, Aerospace, Metal furniture and Manufacturing.
